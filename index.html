Football Overview

Football, also known as soccer in some regions, is the world’s most beloved sport. Played and watched by billions across the globe, it brings together players and fans with a single mission: to score goals and celebrate teamwork.

Each match features two teams of eleven, including a dedicated goalkeeper for each side. The game follows the 17 Laws of the Game, maintained by IFAB and governed worldwide by FIFA.

Games are played on rectangular fields of grass or artificial turf, with goals at either end. A standard match consists of two 45-minute halves, plus extra time when necessary. Except for goalkeepers in their penalty area, players must rely solely on their feet, head, and body and no hands allowed!

Football is celebrated for its creativity, rhythm, and unpredictability. Iconic tournaments like the FIFA World Cup, UEFA Champions League, and Copa América highlight the best talents the sport has to offer. From spontaneous street matches to packed stadium spectacles, football unites cultures and ignites passion like no other.
